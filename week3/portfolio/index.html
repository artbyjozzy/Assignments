About Me

Artist & Model.

I am a pencil, NFT and Digital freelance artist currently based in Nigeria. I also model part time. I love to create art from new ideas and ambitious challenges. So far in less than 5 years I’ve been able to master my craft as an artist and in less than a year I’ve established myself as an NFT artist.
